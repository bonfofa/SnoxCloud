About Us

Snox Cloud is a global cloud gaming provider. We enable one-click cloud-based access to PC video games across devices and platforms. With Snox Cloud, users can run games they own on Windows, MacOS, iOS, Linux, Android and Android TV. We’re also making PC games available not only on PC/Mac/laptops, but on smartphones and TVs. The power of user device no longer matters, as all rendering happens on Snox Cloud custom hardware designed together with world’s leading vendors and optimized for cloud gaming. Gameplay is then streamed to the user device over the Internet. Snox Cloud has one of the world’s widest GPU-infrastructure coverage that helps us deliver low-latency cloud gaming experience to millions of users.
